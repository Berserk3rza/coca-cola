Data privacy and security is a top priority for MiX Telematics. We integrate effortlessly and securely with third party systems to provide you with the data about drivers and fleet.

Your data is securely hosted in one of several top-tier data centres and accessed online through our MiX Fleet Managerplatform. Access rights are pre-determined and managed by our people,

Your data is in safe hands. MiX is fully ISO 27001 compliant as well as adhering to UK Data protection laws and GDPR.
